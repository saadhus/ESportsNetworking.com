How do we work?


Quite simple actually!
1.) Head on our to our application page to sign up to be a partner and join the Network

2.) We get to work using our partner's information to find them sponsors

3.) If you, your team, your tournament/event, or your channel fits a sponsors need, we will contact you to ask if you would like to be part of their campaign.

4.)If you agreed, and did you end of the campaign, then we will thank you and pay you your share of the sponsorship.
