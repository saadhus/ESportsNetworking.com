Partners


What is E-Sports Network?

E-Sports Network is an eSports Management Company, specializing in eSports marketing and sponsorship management. We help the E-Sports industry find sponsors, and we help advertisers successfully build and execute an eSports marketing campaign. For more information, please visit our about page.

How do I partner with E-Sports Network?

Go to our application page, see if you are fit the criteria, and then just sign up! It’s that easy.

How much does it cost to be an ESN partner?

Nothing! We will never take any money from you! And we put no obligations on our partners either! Once a sponsorship opportunity comes along, you can use to be part of it or not, totally up to you!

What's the benefit of joining the Network?

Our entire job is to find sponsors for our partners, so you know we are always on the hunt to get you sponsorship. We only make money when you make money and our sponsors are happy. Our partners are always the first to be notified as soon as we have some opportunities available for you. For more info please visit this page.

How do I make money?

As soon as there is a sponsorship opportunity available, and you fit our sponsor’s needs, we will contact you with details concerning what you need to do, when you should do it, and how much that particular campaign offers. If you agree to take part, and complete your end, then we will send you your share of the sponsorship as soon as we get the money from the sponsors.

How and how much do I get paid?

Each sponsorship is different. We will let you know the exact amount for the particular campaign when we ask if you want to be part of it. If you agree to the terms of that campaign, and finish you end, we will send your share of the sponsorship through PayPal.

How will I know if there is a sponosorship opportunity available?

You are the right fit for our sponsors, we will contact you through the email address you provided. Lots of opportunities have a strict timeline, so make sure you are always checking your email so you don’t miss it.

What about my current sponsors?

They can keep sponsoring you! You can keep the deal you have with them going as is. However, our team has experience dealing with sponsors and knows how to get the best results for both the sponsors and you. So if, and only if, you wish, we can also handle your current sponsors and try to get you more benefits.

Do I have to interact with the sponsors?

No, there’s no need. We handle everything on that end. All you are responsible for doing is following the terms of the campaign and doing your end. We handle all business, legal, financial stuff with the sponsors and, at the end, give you your share.
