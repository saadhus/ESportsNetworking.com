Why Sponsor E-Sports?


Target a wide variety of demographics


By advertising on E-Sports, sponsors can target many, and often times, hard to reach demographics.

E-Sports is traditionally consumed by mostly 12-35 year old males globally, but with its rapid increase in popularity, its gaining new and much more varied audiences from all over the world. Men and women of all ages are enjoying E-Sports more commonly everyday.

Reach audiences all over the world at once


E-Sports is a global phenomenon and is popular all over the world. Therefore, sponsoring E-Sports means that audiences all over the world will be able to see your message.

Because E-Sports is mostly online, even small casts and local tournaments have the ability to be see my millions of people all over the world


Customize your sponsorship


Sponsor large global tournaments or smaller local events, or anything in between. E-Sports gives you huge customizability options reagarding sponsorship.

Additionally, you can choose to sponsor an E-Sports tournament, E-Sports casts and caster, Professional Gamers or even a combination of all three. There are many different sponsorship levels in E-Sports and you can pick and choose which demographics to hit and how much to sponsor

Get early footing on a booming market


While E-Sports had humble begginings, that is no longer the case. E-Sports is booming in popularity all over the world, and is quickly becoming the go-to entertainment venue for the new generation.

E-Sports has garnered millions of fans across the world, becoming a household name in many parts of the globe. And it still continues to grow, along with any sponsorhip invested in it. By sponsoring E-Sports and advertising your brand or product on it not only are you investing in E-Sports now, but investing in its continual growth, which is sure to pay dividends in the future.
