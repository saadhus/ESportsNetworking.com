Who are we?




Simply put, we manage eSports sponsorships.


But really, we are a lot more than that.
We help our partners find sponsorships, act as an agent for Pro Players and represent our partnered tournaments and events in front of corporate sponsors. We handle all the befores and afters that come with sponsorships, and only give you what you need and your share of the sponsorship.

For our sponsors we do all that you need. We can make you an eSports marketing plan, run a campaign on your behalf and give you all the results. We’ve got it all under one roof. If you want to advertise to the gaming crowd, then you have come to the right place.


eSports Network was founded in 2011
By die-hard fans who saw the awesome potential of the eSports market, and wanted nothing more than to help it grow. With extensive backgrounds in marketing, financial analysis, and business management, we left corporate America, and went to work using our skills and experiences to help eSports.


We have big goal
To help the eSports market boom. To that extend we need your help. The more partners we have, the more sponsors we can get. More sponsors equals more money in eSports which equals bigger and better eSports which attracts more fans. And at the end of the day, that is what we really want, to give the fans a better show.
