What is E-Sports?

Who are we, and what does do?

Simply put, we specializes in marketing for the E-Sports industry.


But really, we are a lot more than that. We are your one-stop-shop for all your E-Sports needs. Whether you are a looking to promote your brand name or product through a well specified medium, or an E-Sports brand looking for sponsors to take advantage of what you offer, we have you covered.


E-Sports Network was founded in 2011 by individuals experienced in both the corporate and E-Sports world. Our founding goals were simple, help people recognize and take advantage of all the wonderful opportunities the E-Sports media world offers. It’s some simple goals, but ones we strive quite hard towards. We are passionate about what we do, we love the E-Sports world believe the untapped potential behind it is enormous, to say the least. We love helping sponsors find out exactly what they need to grow their brand in E-Sports and love helping the E-Sports community by finding it sponsors.


We offer a broad range of services including, sponsorship, live and online event management, promotions, trials and community development programs, to name a few. We can reach gamers at many different touch points and in many different ways: individually, in groups, in-game as well as outside the game play experience.
