What is the ESN Partners Network?




The ESN Partners Network is made up of some of the best eSports organizations, tournaments, teams, and players that are looking to bring their game to the next level through sponsorship.

At ESN our job is to find sponsorship for our partners, and that’s exactly what we do. We are constantly representing our partners’ potential in front of possible sponsors, and looking through every brand to find advertisers that fit.

Our Network Partners not only have us hunting for new sponsors on their behalf, but also have sponsorship made simple. We handle all the market planning, negotiating, financials and responsibilities that come with dealing with sponsors, and give you just what you need, and your share of the sponsorship.



How does it work?



1.) Join the Network


Go down below to our application form, fill it out and submit it.

While it may be called an application form, don’t worry, we probably think you are good enough. Pretty much all you have to do is meet our minimal criteria and we’ll welcome you with open arms. Once you’ve submitted the application, we are going to give it a quick review, and then send you an email welcoming you to the Network.


2.) We get to work


Now that you've joined the Network, you can rest easy knowing that we are working hard trying to find you sponsors.

We've got a huge set of contacts, and friendly relations with a lot of the sponsors out there. So, we are constantly showing them the potentials of our new partners and seeing if you would be the right fit for their brand.


3.) As soon as we get a sponsorship campaign going, we'll contact you.


If we find a sponsor who is looking for just the type of eSports person you are to promote their brand, we'll contact you and ask if you want to partake in the campaign.

Not all sponsors might be looking for someone fitting your description to help advertise their brand, but if we get one that is, we will get in touch with you. We will provide you with the detials on that particular campaign, what you have to do and what your share of the sponsorship will be. If you like what you see and agree to take part, we will galdly accept your help.


4.) Get paid for your part


If you agreed to take part in the campaign, and did your part, then we will send you your share of the sponsorship.

At the advent of our sponsors campaign we will send through PayPal your entire share of the sponsorship. What that exactly would be we will let you know when we initially ask if you want to take part in the campaign. However, more then just compensation, you will get our, and our sponsors eternal thanks for helping us out.





Why Join?




It's cost-free, risk-free, and obligation-free!

It's free and easy to join the Network! We will never take any money from you, we won’t ask you to do anything you don’t want, heck, we won’t even spam you with emails. And it’s easy to join, just head on our to our application page, fill it out, send it and that it! We’ll get to work mustering up those sponsorship dollars for you.

Have someone always looking out for you!

Whether you have a small sponsorship department, or are out there by your lonesome, by joining the ESN Partners Network, you know that there is always someone looking out for your sponsorship needs. We only get paid when you get paid through sponsorship, and that’s motivation enough for us to get you as many advertisers as we can.


Get paid when you participate!

Once we find a sponsor that is right for you, and you decide to take part in that sponsorship campaign, we will make sure you get compensated for you efforts! You don’t have to change what you are doing, or even do much extra. Just fulfill the requirements of the campaign, sit back and enjoy your share of the sponsorship.

Be the first to be alerted about new sponsorships!

Being part of the Network means that you will get immediately alerted to any new sponsorship we have available. We put our partners first, and we make sure they are the first ones to get a crack at any sponsorship that comes our way, even if the sponsor has limited space or limited funds available.
